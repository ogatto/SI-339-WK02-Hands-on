About Me

I am a senior currently enrolled in the School of Information at The University of Michigan. I grew up in the suburbs of New York about 45 minutes outside of Manhattan. In my free time I enjoy almost anything related to sports and love to play Ice Hockey and Lacrosse. One of my favorite things to do is also try new restaurants, cook, and travel.

The previous two summers I have worked in New York City for Stifel Financial Corp. and ESPN. Both of these internships were incredible learning experiences and formative for my professional development. But since I was young I have always wanted to work in the technology industry. My work experiences combined with the cirriculum of the BSI program have led me to pursue a full-time job position at IBM working in Technical Sales and Consulting.
